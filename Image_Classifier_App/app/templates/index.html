Semi-Intelligent Image Classifier
This is an image classification app that lets you train and test your own SVM classifiers. Get started by using one of the Featured Classifiers to classify your images, and then create your own custom classifier. These classifiers learn from the mean and standard deviation of the RGB pixel values of the images, so they have limited potential since they only learn from the colors of the image. Still, you may be surprised by what they can accomplish!
